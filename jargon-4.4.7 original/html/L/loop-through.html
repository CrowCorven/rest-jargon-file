loop through `Prev `__  L  `Next `__ -------------- **loop through**: vt. To process each element of a list of things. “Hold on, I've got to loop through my paper mail.” Derives from the computer-language notion of an iterative loop; compare cdr down (under `*cdr* <../C/cdr.html>`__), which is less common among C and Unix programmers.
