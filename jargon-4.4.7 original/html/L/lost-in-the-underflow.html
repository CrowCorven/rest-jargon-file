lost in the underflow `Prev `__  L  `Next `__ -------------- **lost in the underflow**: adj. Too small to be worth considering; more specifically, small beyond the limits of accuracy or measurement. This is a reference to floating underflow, a condition that can occur when a floating-point arithmetic processor tries to handle quantities smaller than its limit of magnitude.
