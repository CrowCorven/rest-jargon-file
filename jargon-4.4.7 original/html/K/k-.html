k- `Prev `__  K  `Next `__ -------------- **k-**: pref. [rare; poss fr. kilo- prefix] Extremely. Rare among hackers, but quite common among crackers and `*warez d00dz* <../W/warez-d00dz.html>`__ in compounds such as k-kool /K´kool´/, k-rad /K´rad´/, and k-awesome /K´aw\`sm/. Also used to intensify negatives; thus, k-evil, k-lame, k-screwed, and k-annoying.
