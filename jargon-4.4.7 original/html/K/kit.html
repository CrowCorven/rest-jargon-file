kit `Prev `__  K  `Next `__ -------------- **kit**: n. [Usenet; poss.: fr.: `*DEC* <../D/DEC.html>`__ slang for a full software distribution, as opposed to a patch or upgrade] A source software distribution that has been packaged in such a way that it can (theoretically) be unpacked and installed according to a series of steps using only standard Unix tools, and entirely documented by some reasonable chain of references from the top-level `*README file* <../R/README-file.html>`__.
