ken `Prev `__  K  `Next `__ -------------- **ken**: /ken/, n. 1. [Unix] Ken Thompson, principal inventor of Unix. In the early days he used to hand-cut distribution tapes, often with a note that read “Love, ken”. Old-timers still use his first name (sometimes uncapitalized, because it's a login name and mail address) in third-person reference; it is widely understood (on Usenet, in particular) that without a last name ‘Ken’ refers only to Ken Thompson. Similarly, ‘Dennis’ without last name means Dennis Ritchie (and he is often known as ``dmr``). See also `*demigod* <../D/demigod.html>`__, `*Unix* <../U/Unix.html>`__. 2. A flaming user.
