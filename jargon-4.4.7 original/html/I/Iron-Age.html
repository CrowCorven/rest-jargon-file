Iron Age `Prev `__  I  `Next `__ -------------- **Iron Age**: n. In the history of computing, 1961-1971 — the formative era of commercial `*mainframe* <../M/mainframe.html>`__ technology, when ferrite-core `*dinosaur* <../D/dinosaur.html>`__\ s ruled the earth. The Iron Age began, ironically enough, with the delivery of the first minicomputer (the PDP-1) and ended with the introduction of the first commercial microprocessor (the Intel 4004) in 1971.
