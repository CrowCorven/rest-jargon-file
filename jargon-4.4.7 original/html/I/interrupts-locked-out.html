interrupts locked out `Prev `__  I  `Next `__ -------------- **interrupts locked out**: adj. When someone is ignoring you. In a restaurant, after several fruitless attempts to get the waitress's attention, a hacker might well observe “She must have interrupts locked out”. The synonym interrupts disabled is also common. Variations abound; “to have one's interrupt mask bit set” and “interrupts masked out” are also heard.
