interrupt `Prev `__  I  `Next `__ -------------- **interrupt** 1. [techspeak] n. On a computer, an event that interrupts normal processing and temporarily diverts flow-of-control through an “interrupt handler” routine. See also `*trap* <../T/trap.html>`__. 2. interj. A request for attention from a hacker.
