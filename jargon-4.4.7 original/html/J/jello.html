jello `Prev `__  J  `Next `__ -------------- **jello**: n. [Usenet: by analogy with `*spam* <../S/spam.html>`__] A message that is both excessively cross-posted and too frequently posted, as opposed to `*spam* <../S/spam.html>`__ (which is merely too frequently posted) or `*velveeta* <../V/velveeta.html>`__ (which is merely excessively cross-posted).
