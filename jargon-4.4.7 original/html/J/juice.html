juice `Prev `__  J  `Next `__ -------------- **juice**: n. The weight of a given node in some sort of graph (like a web of trust or a relevance-weighted search query). This appears to have been generalized from `*google juice* <../G/google-juice.html>`__, but may derive from black urban slang for power or a respect.
