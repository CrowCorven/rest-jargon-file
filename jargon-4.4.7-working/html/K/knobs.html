knobs `Prev `__  K  `Next `__ -------------- **knobs**: pl.n. Configurable options, even in software and even those you can't adjust in real time. Anything you can `*twiddle* <../T/twiddle.html>`__ is a knob. “Has this PNG viewer got an alpha knob?” Software may be described as having “knobs and switches” or occasionally “knobs and lights”.
