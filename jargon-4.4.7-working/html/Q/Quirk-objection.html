Quirk objection `Prev `__  Q  `Next `__ -------------- **Quirk objection**: interj. [Named for Captain Gym Z. Quirk, the first to raise it.] “Objection! Assumes organ not in evidence!” Used in ``news.admin.net-abuse.email`` to point out that a comment assumes the presence of something whose existence has not been proven, such as a spammer's brain or gonads. This is not used to refer to things that are definitely proven *not* to exist, such as a spammer's ethics.
