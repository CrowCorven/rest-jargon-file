video toaster `Prev `__  V  `Next `__ -------------- **video toaster**: n. Historically, an Amiga fitted with a particular line of special video effects hardware from NewTek — long a popular platform at special-effects and video production houses. More generally, any computer system designed specifically for video production and manipulation.
