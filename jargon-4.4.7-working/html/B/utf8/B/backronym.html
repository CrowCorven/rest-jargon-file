backronym `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **backronym**: n. [portmanteau of back + acronym] A word interpreted as an acronym that was not originally so intended. This is a special case of what linguists call back formation. Examples are given under `*recursive acronym* <../R/recursive-acronym.html>`__ (Cygnus), `*Acme* <../A/Acme.html>`__, and `*mung* <../M/mung.html>`__. Discovering backronyms is a common form of wordplay among hackers.
