benchmark `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **benchmark**: n. [techspeak] An inaccurate measure of computer performance. “In the computer industry, there are three kinds of lies: lies, damn lies, and benchmarks.” Well-known ones include Whetstone, Dhrystone, Rhealstone (see `*h* <../H/h.html>`__), the Gabriel LISP benchmarks, the SPECmark suite, and LINPACK.
