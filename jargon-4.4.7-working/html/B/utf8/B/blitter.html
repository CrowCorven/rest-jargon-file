blitter `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **blitter**: /blit´r/, n. [common] A special-purpose chip or hardware system built to perform `*blit* `__ operations, esp. used for fast implementation of bit-mapped graphics. The Commodore Amiga and a few other micros have these, but since 1990 the trend has been away from them (however, see `*cycle of reincarnation* <../C/cycle-of-reincarnation.html>`__).
