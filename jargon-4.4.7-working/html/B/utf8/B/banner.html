banner `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **banner**: n. 1. A top-centered graphic on a web page. Esp. used in `*banner ad* `__. 2. On interactive software, a first screen containing a logo and/or author credits and/or a copyright notice. Similar to `*splash screen* <../S/splash-screen.html>`__. 3. The title page added to printouts by most print spoolers (see `*spool* <../S/spool.html>`__). Typically includes user or account ID information in very large character-graphics capitals. Also called a burst page, because it indicates where to burst (tear apart) fanfold paper to separate one user's printout from the next. 4.
