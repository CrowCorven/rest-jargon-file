BLT `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **BLT**: /B·L·T/, /bl@t/, /belt/, n.,vt. Synonym for `*blit* `__. This is the original form of `*blit* `__ and the ancestor of `*bitblt* `__. It referred to any large bit-field copy or move operation (one resource-intensive memory-shuffling operation done on pre-paged versions of ITS, WAITS, and TOPS-10 was sardonically referred to as “The Big BLT”).
