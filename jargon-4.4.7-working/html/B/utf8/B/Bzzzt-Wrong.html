Bzzzt! Wrong. `Prev `__  B  `Next <../C.html>`__ -------------- **Bzzzt! Wrong.**: /bzt rong/, excl. [common; Usenet/Internet; punctuation varies] From a Robin Williams routine in the movie *Dead Poets Society* spoofing radio or TV quiz programs, such as *Truth or Consequences*, where an incorrect answer earns one a blast from the buzzer and condolences from the interlocutor. A way of expressing mock-rude disagreement, usually immediately following an included quote from another poster.
