Black Screen of Death `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **Black Screen of Death**: [prob.: related to the Floating Head of Death in a famous *Far Side* cartoon.] A failure mode of `*Microsloth Windows* <../M/Microsloth-Windows.html>`__. On an attempt to launch a DOS box, a networked Windows system not uncommonly blanks the screen and locks up the PC so hard that it requires a cold `*boot* `__ to recover. This unhappy phenomenon is known as The Black Screen of Death.
