bomb `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **bomb** 1. v. General synonym for `*crash* <../C/crash.html>`__ (sense 1) except that it is not used as a noun; esp. used of software or OS failures. “Don't run Empire with less than 32K stack, it'll bomb.” 2. n.,v. Atari ST and Macintosh equivalents of a Unix panic or Amiga `*guru meditation* <../G/guru-meditation.html>`__, in which icons of little black-powder bombs or mushroom clouds are displayed, indicating that the system has died. On the Mac, this may be accompanied by a decimal (or occasionally hexadecimal) number indicating what went wrong, similar to the Amiga `*guru meditation* <../G/guru-meditation.html>`__ number. `*MS-DOS* <../M/MS-DOS.html>`__ machines tend to get `*locked up* <../L/locked-up.html>`__ in this situation. -------------- +--------------------------+----------------------------+----------------------------------------------------+ | `Prev `__  | `Up <../B.html>`__ |  `Next `__ | +--------------------------+----------------------------+----------------------------------------------------+ | boink  | `Home <../index.html>`__ |  bondage-and-discipline language | +--------------------------+----------------------------+----------------------------------------------------+
