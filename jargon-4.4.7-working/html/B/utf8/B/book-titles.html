book titles `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **book titles** There is a tradition in hackerdom of informally tagging important textbooks and standards documents with the dominant color of their covers or with some other conspicuous feature of the cover. Many of these are described in this lexicon under their own entries. See `*Aluminum Book* <../A/Aluminum-Book.html>`__, `*Camel Book* <../C/Camel-Book.html>`__, `*Cinderella Book* <../C/Cinderella-Book.html>`__, `*daemon book* <../D/daemon-book.html>`__, `*Dragon Book* <../D/Dragon-Book.html>`__, `*Orange Book* <../O/Orange-Book.html>`__, `*Purple Book* <../P/Purple-Book.html>`__, `*Wizard Book* <../W/Wizard-Book.html>`__, and `*bible* `__; see also `*rainbow series* <../R/rainbow-series.html>`__.
