brain dump `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **brain dump**: n. [common] The act of telling someone everything one knows about a particular topic or project. Typically used when someone is going to let a new party maintain a piece of code. Conceptually analogous to an operating system `*core dump* <../C/core-dump.html>`__ in that it saves a lot of useful `*state* <../S/state.html>`__ before an exit. “You'll have to give me a brain dump on FOOBAR before you start your new job at HackerCorp.” See `*core dump* <../C/core-dump.html>`__ (sense 4).
