bozotic `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **bozotic**: /boh·zoh´tik/, /boh·zo´tik/, adj. [from the name of a TV clown even more losing than Ronald McDonald] Resembling or having the quality of a bozo; that is, clownish, ludicrously wrong, unintentionally humorous. Compare `*wonky* <../W/wonky.html>`__, `*demented* <../D/demented.html>`__.
