bitblt `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **bitblt**: /bit´blit/, n. [from `*BLT* `__, q.v.:] 1. [common] Any of a family of closely related algorithms for moving and copying rectangles of bits between main and display memory on a bit-mapped device, or between two areas of either main or display memory (the requirement to do the `*Right Thing* <../R/Right-Thing.html>`__ in the case of overlapping source and destination rectangles is what makes BitBlt tricky). 2. Synonym for `*blit* `__ or `*BLT* `__.
