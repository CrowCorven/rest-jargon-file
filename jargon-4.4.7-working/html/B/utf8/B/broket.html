broket `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **broket**: /broh´k@t/, /broh´ket\`/, n. [rare; by analogy with ‘bracket’: a ‘broken bracket’] Either of the characters ``<`` and ``>``, when used as paired enclosing delimiters. This word originated as a contraction of the phrase ‘broken bracket’, that is, a bracket that is bent in the middle.
