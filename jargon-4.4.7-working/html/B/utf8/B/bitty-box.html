bitty box `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **bitty box**: /bit´ee boks/, n. 1. A computer sufficiently small, primitive, or incapable as to cause a hacker acute claustrophobia at the thought of developing software on or for it. Especially used of small, obsolescent, single-tasking-only personal machines such as the Atari 800, Osborne, Sinclair, VIC-20, TRS-80, or IBM PC. 2. [Pejorative] More generally, the opposite of ‘real computer’ (see `*Get a real computer!* <../G/Get-a-real-computer-.html>`__).
