biff `Prev `__  B  `Next `__ -------------- **biff**: /bif/, vt. [now rare] To notify someone of incoming mail. From the BSD utility biff(1), which was in turn named after a friendly dog who used to chase frisbees in the halls at UCB while 4.2BSD was in development. There was a legend that it had a habit of barking whenever the mailman came, but the author of **biff** says this is not true.
