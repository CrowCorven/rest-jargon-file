molly-guard `Prev `__  M  `Next `__ -------------- **molly-guard**: /mol´ee·gard/, n. [University of Illinois] A shield to prevent tripping of some `*Big Red Switch* <../B/Big-Red-Switch.html>`__ by clumsy or ignorant hands. Originally used of the plexiglass covers improvised for the BRS on an IBM 4341 after a programmer's toddler daughter (named Molly) frobbed it twice in one day. Later generalized to covers over stop/reset switches on disk drives and networking equipment.
