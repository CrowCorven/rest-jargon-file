macrology `Prev `__  M  `Next `__ -------------- **macrology**: /mak·rol'@·jee/, n. 1. Set of usually complex or crufty macros, e.g., as part of a large system written in `*LISP* <../L/LISP.html>`__, `*TECO* <../T/TECO.html>`__, or (less commonly) assembler. 2. The art and science involved in comprehending a macrology in sense 1. Sometimes studying the macrology of a system is not unlike archeology, ecology, or `*theology* <../T/theology.html>`__, hence the sound-alike construction.
