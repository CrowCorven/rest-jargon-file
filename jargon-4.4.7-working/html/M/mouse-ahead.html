mouse ahead `Prev `__  M  `Next `__ -------------- **mouse ahead**: vi. Point-and-click analog of type ahead. To manipulate a computer's pointing device (almost always a mouse in this usage, but not necessarily) and its selection or command buttons before a computer program is ready to accept such input, in anticipation of the program accepting the input.
