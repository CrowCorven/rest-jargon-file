mandelbug `Prev `__  M  `Next `__ -------------- **mandelbug**: /man´del·buhg/, n. [from the Mandelbrot set] A bug whose underlying causes are so complex and obscure as to make its behavior appear chaotic or even non-deterministic. This term implies that the speaker thinks it is a `*Bohr bug* <../B/Bohr-bug.html>`__, rather than a `*heisenbug* <../H/heisenbug.html>`__.
