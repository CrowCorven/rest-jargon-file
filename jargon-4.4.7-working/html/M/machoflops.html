machoflops `Prev `__  M  `Next `__ -------------- **machoflops**: /mach´oh·flops/, n. [pun on megaflops, a coinage for ‘millions of FLoating-point Operations Per Second’] Refers to artificially inflated performance figures often quoted by computer manufacturers. Real applications are lucky to get half the quoted speed.
