mailbomb `Prev `__  M  `Next `__ -------------- **mailbomb** (also mail bomb) [Usenet] 1. v. To send, or urge others to send, massive amounts of `*email* <../E/email.html>`__ to a single system or person, esp. with intent to crash or `*spam* <../S/spam.html>`__ the recipient's system. Sometimes done in retaliation for a perceived serious offense. Mailbombing is itself widely regarded as a serious offense — it can disrupt email traffic or other facilities for innocent users on the victim's system, and in extreme cases, even at upstream sites. 2. n. An automatic procedure with a similar effect. 3. n. The mail sent.
