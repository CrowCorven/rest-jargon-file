middle-endian `Prev `__  M  `Next `__ -------------- **middle-endian**: adj. Not `*big-endian* <../B/big-endian.html>`__ or `*little-endian* <../L/little-endian.html>`__. Used of perverse byte orders such as 3-4-1-2 or 2-1-4-3, occasionally found in the packed-decimal formats of minicomputer manufacturers who shall remain nameless. See `*NUXI problem* <../N/NUXI-problem.html>`__.
