munchkin `Prev `__  M  `Next `__ -------------- **munchkin**: /muhnch´kin/, n. [from the squeaky-voiced little people in L. Frank Baum's *The Wizard of Oz*] A teenage-or-younger micro enthusiast hacking BASIC or something else equally constricted. A term of mild derision — munchkins are annoying but some grow up to be hackers after passing through a `*larval stage* <../L/larval-stage.html>`__. The term `*urchin* <../U/urchin.html>`__ is also used.
