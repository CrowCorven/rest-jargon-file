management `Prev `__  M  `Next `__ -------------- **management**: n. 1. Corporate power elites distinguished primarily by their distance from actual productive work and their chronic failure to manage (see also `*suit* <../S/suit.html>`__). Spoken derisively, as in “\ *Management* decided that ...”. 2. Mythically, a vast bureaucracy responsible for all the world's minor irritations.
