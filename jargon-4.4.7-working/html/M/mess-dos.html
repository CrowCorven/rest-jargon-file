mess-dos `Prev `__  M  `Next `__ -------------- **mess-dos**: /mes·dos/, n. [semi-obsolescent now that DOS is] Derisory term for MS-DOS. Often followed by the ritual banishing “Just say No!” See `*MS-DOS* `__. Most hackers (even many MS-DOS hackers) loathed MS-DOS for its single-tasking nature, its limits on application size, its nasty primitive interface, and its ties to IBMness and Microsoftness (see `*fear and loathing* <../F/fear-and-loathing.html>`__). Also mess-loss, messy-dos, mess-dog, mess-dross, mush-dos, and various combinations thereof.
