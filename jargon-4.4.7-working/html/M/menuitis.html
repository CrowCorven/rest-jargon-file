menuitis `Prev `__  M  `Next `__ -------------- **menuitis**: /men\`yoo·i:´tis/, n. Notional disease suffered by software with an obsessively simple-minded menu interface and no escape. Hackers find this intensely irritating and much prefer the flexibility of command-line or language-style interfaces, especially those customizable via macros or a special-purpose language in which one can encode useful hacks.
