moria `Prev `__  M  `Next `__ -------------- **moria**: /mor´ee·@/, n. Like `*nethack* <../N/nethack.html>`__ and `*rogue* <../R/rogue.html>`__, one of the large PD Dungeons-and-Dragons-like simulation games, available for a wide range of machines and operating systems. The name is from Tolkien's Mines of Moria; compare `*elder days* <../E/elder-days.html>`__, `*elvish* <../E/elvish.html>`__. The game is extremely addictive and a major consumer of time better used for hacking.
