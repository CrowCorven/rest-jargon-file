marketroid `Prev `__  M  `Next `__ -------------- **marketroid**: /mar´k@·troyd/, n. alt.: marketing slime, marketeer, mar­ket­ing droid, marketdroid. A member of a company's marketing department, esp. one who promises users that the next version of a product will have features that are not actually scheduled for inclusion, are extremely difficult to implement, and/or are in violation of the laws of physics; and/or one who describes existing features (and misfeatures) in ebullient, buzzword-laden adspeak. Derogatory.
