off-by-one error `Prev `__  O  `Next `__ -------------- **off-by-one error**: n. [common] Exceedingly common error induced in many ways, such as by starting at 0 when you should have started at 1 or vice-versa, or by writing **< N** instead of **<= N** or vice-versa. Also applied to giving something to the person next to the one who should have gotten it.
