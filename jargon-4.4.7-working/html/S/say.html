say `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **say**: vt. 1. To type to a terminal. “To list a directory verbosely, you have to say **ls -l**.” Tends to imply a `*newline* <../N/newline.html>`__-terminated command (a ‘sentence’). 2. A computer may also be said to ‘say’ things to you, even if it doesn't have a speech synthesizer, by displaying them on a terminal in response to your commands.
