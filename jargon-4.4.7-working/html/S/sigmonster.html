sigmonster `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **sigmonster**: n. [common] A beast that randomly chooses one of a selection of signatures for appending to mail and news messages. The creature is most often mentioned directly when it has been in particularly good form and selected a signature appropriate to the topic being discussed; the construction “P.S.: good sigmonster, have a cookie” is not uncommon.
