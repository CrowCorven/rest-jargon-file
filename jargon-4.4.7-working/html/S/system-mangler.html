system mangler `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **system mangler**: n. Humorous synonym for ‘system manager’, poss. from the fact that one major IBM OS had a `*root* <../R/root.html>`__ account called SYSMANGR. Refers specifically to a systems programmer in charge of administration, software maintenance, and updates at some site.
