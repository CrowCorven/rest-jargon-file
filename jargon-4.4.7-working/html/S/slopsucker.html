slopsucker `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **slopsucker**: /slop´suhk·r/, n. A lowest-priority task that waits around until everything else has ‘had its fill’ of machine resources. Only when the machine would otherwise be idle is the task allowed to \`‘suck up the slop’. Also called a hungry puppy or bottom feeder. One common variety of slopsucker hunts for large prime numbers.
