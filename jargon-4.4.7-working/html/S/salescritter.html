salescritter `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **salescritter**: /sayls´kri\`tr/, n. Pejorative hackerism for a computer salesperson. Hackers tell the following joke: | | Q. What's the difference between a used-car dealer and a |    computer salesman? | A. The used-car dealer knows he's lying.  [Some versions add: |    ...and probably knows how to drive.] This reflects the widespread hacker belief that salescritters are self-selected for stupidity (after all, if they had brains and the inclination to use them, they'd be in programming). The terms salesthing and salesdroid are also common.
