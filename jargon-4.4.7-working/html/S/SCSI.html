SCSI `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **SCSI**: n. [Small Computer System Interface] A bus-independent standard for system-level interfacing between a computer and intelligent devices. Typically annotated in literature with ‘sexy’ (/sek´see/), ‘sissy’ (/sis´ee/), and ‘scuzzy’ (/skuh´zee/) as pronunciation guides — the last being the overwhelmingly predominant form, much to the dismay of the designers and their marketing people.
