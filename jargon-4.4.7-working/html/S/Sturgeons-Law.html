Sturgeon's Law `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **Sturgeon's Law**: prov. “Ninety percent of everything is crud”. Derived from a quote by science fiction author Theodore Sturgeon, who once said, “Sure, 90% of science fiction is crud. That's because 90% of everything is crud.” Sturgeon himself called this “Sturgeon's Revelation”, and it first appeared in the March 1958 issue of *Venture Science Fiction*; he gave Sturgeon's Law as “Nothing is always absolutely so.” Oddly, when Sturgeon's Revelation is cited, the final word is almost invariably changed to ‘crap’. Compare `*Hanlon's Razor* <../H/Hanlons-Razor.html>`__, `*Ninety-Ninety Rule* <../N/Ninety-Ninety-Rule.html>`__.
