sig virus `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **sig virus**: n. A parasitic `*meme* <../M/meme.html>`__ embedded in a `*sig block* `__. There was a `*meme plague* <../M/meme-plague.html>`__ or fad for these on Usenet in late 1991. Most were equivalents of “I am a .sig virus. Please reproduce me in your .sig block.”. Of course, the .sig virus's memetic hook is the giggle value of going along with the gag; this, however, was a self-limiting phenomenon as more and more people picked up on the idea.
