social engineering `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **social engineering**: n. Term used among `*cracker* <../C/cracker.html>`__\ s and `*samurai* `__ for cracking techniques that rely on weaknesses in `*wetware* <../W/wetware.html>`__ rather than software; the aim is to trick people into revealing passwords or other information that compromises a target system's security. Classic scams include phoning up a mark who has the required information and posing as a field service tech or a fellow employee with an urgent access problem.
