spoiler space `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **spoiler space** [also spoiler spoo or spoiler protection] A screenful of blank or spacer lines deliberately inserted in a message following a `*spoiler* `__ warning, so the actual spoiler can't be seen without hitting a key.
