snarf & barf `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **snarf & barf**: /snarf´n·barf\`/, n. Under a `*WIMP environment* <../W/WIMP-environment.html>`__, the act of grabbing a region of text and then stuffing the contents of that region into another region (or the same one) to avoid retyping a command line.
