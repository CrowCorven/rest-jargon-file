STR `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **STR** Spot the reference. Used in `*scary devil monastery* `__ to mark the witticism one just uttered as a quote from some work of art or literature, the more obscure the better. Those who know where the reference comes from reply in the form “You are $CHARACTER, and you owe me $ITEM”, where $CHARACTER is a character from the story being referenced and $ITEM is something associated with that character.
