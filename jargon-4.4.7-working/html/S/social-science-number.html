social science number `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **social science number**: n., // [IBM] A statistic that is `*content-free* <../C/content-free.html>`__, or nearly so. A measure derived via methods of questionable validity from data of a dubious and vague nature. Predictively, having a social science number in hand is seldom much better than nothing, and can be considerably worse. As a rule, `*management* <../M/management.html>`__ loves them.
