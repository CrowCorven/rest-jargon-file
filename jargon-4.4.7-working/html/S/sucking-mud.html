sucking mud `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **sucking mud**: adj. [Applied Data Research] (also pumping mud) Crashed or `*wedged* <../W/wedged.html>`__. Usually said of a machine that provides some service to a network, such as a file server. This Dallas regionalism derives from the East Texas oilfield lament, “Shut 'er down, Ma, she's a-suckin' mud”.
