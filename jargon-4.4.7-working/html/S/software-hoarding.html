software hoarding `Prev `__  S  `Next `__ -------------- **software hoarding**: n. Pejorative term employed by members and adherents of the `*GNU* <../G/GNU.html>`__ project to describe the act of holding software proprietary, keeping it under trade secret or license terms which prohibit free redistribution and modification. Used primarily in Free Software Foundation propaganda.
