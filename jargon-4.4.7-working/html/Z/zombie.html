zombie `Prev `__  Z  `Next `__ -------------- **zombie**: n. 1. [Unix] A process that has died but has not yet relinquished its process table slot (because the parent process hasn't executed a wait(2) for it yet). These can be seen in ps(1) listings occasionally. Compare `*orphan* <../O/orphan.html>`__. 2. A machine, especially someone's `*home box* <../H/home-box.html>`__, that has been cracked and is being used as part of a second-stage attack by miscreants trying to mask their home IP address.
