zapped `Prev `__  Z  `Next `__ -------------- **zapped**: adj. Spicy. This term is used to distinguish between food that is hot (in temperature) and food that is *spicy*-hot. For example, the Chinese appetizer Bon Bon Chicken is a kind of chicken salad that is cold but zapped; by contrast, `*vanilla* <../V/vanilla.html>`__ wonton soup is hot but not zapped. See also `*oriental food* <../O/oriental-food.html>`__, `*laser chicken* <../L/laser-chicken.html>`__.
