link-dead `Prev `__  L  `Next `__ -------------- **link-dead**: adj. [MUD] The state a player is in when they kill their connection to a `*MUD* <../M/MUD.html>`__ without leaving it properly. The player is then commonly left as a statue in the game, and is only removed after a certain period of time (an hour on most MUDs). Used on `*IRC* <../I/IRC.html>`__ as well, although it is inappropriate in that context.
