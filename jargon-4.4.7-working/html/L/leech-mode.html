leech mode `Prev `__  L  `Next `__ -------------- **leech mode**: n. [warez d00dz] “Leech mode” or “leech access” or (simply “leech” as in “You get leech”) is the access mode on a FTP site where one can download as many files as one wants, without having to upload. Leech mode is often promised on banner sites, but rarely obtained.
