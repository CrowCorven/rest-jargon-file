leaf site `Prev `__  L  `Next `__ -------------- **leaf site**: n. [obs.] Before pervasive TCP/IP, this term was used of a machine that merely originated and read Usenet news or mail, and did not relay any third-party traffic. It was often uttered in a critical tone; when the ratio of leaf sites to backbone, rib, and other relay sites got too high, the network tended to develop bottlenecks. Compare `*backbone site* <../B/backbone-site.html>`__.
