priesthood `Prev `__  P  `Next `__ -------------- **priesthood**: n. [TMRC; obs.] The select group of system managers responsible for the operation and maintenance of a batch computer system. On these computers, a user never had direct access to a computer, but had to submit his/her data and programs to a priest for execution.
