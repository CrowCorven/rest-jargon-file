point-and-drool interface `Prev `__  P  `Next `__ -------------- **point-and-drool interface**: n. Parody of the techspeak term point-and-click interface, describing a windows, icons, and mouse-based interface such as is found on the Macintosh. The implication, of course, is that such an interface is only suitable for idiots.
