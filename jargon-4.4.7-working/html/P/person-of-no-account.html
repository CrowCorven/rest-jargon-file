person of no account `Prev `__  P  `Next `__ -------------- **person of no account**: n. [University of California at Santa Cruz] Used when referring to a person with no `*network address* <../N/network-address.html>`__, frequently to forestall confusion. Most often as part of an introduction: “This is Bill, a person of no account, but he used to be bill@random.com”.
