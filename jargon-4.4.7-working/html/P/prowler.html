prowler `Prev `__  P  `Next `__ -------------- **prowler**: n. [Unix] A `*daemon* <../D/daemon.html>`__ that is run periodically (typically once a week) to seek out and erase `*core* <../C/core.html>`__ files, truncate administrative logfiles, nuke **lost+found** directories, and otherwise clean up the `*cruft* <../C/cruft.html>`__ that tends to pile up in the corners of a file system.
