plug-and-pray `Prev `__  P  `Next `__ -------------- **plug-and-pray**: adj.,vi. Parody of the techspeak term plug-and-play, describing a PC peripheral card which is claimed to have no need for hardware configuration via jumpers or DIP switches, and which should work as soon as it is inserted in the PC.
