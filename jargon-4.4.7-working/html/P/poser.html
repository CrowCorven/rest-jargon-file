poser `Prev `__  P  `Next `__ -------------- **poser**: n. [from French *poseur*] A `*wannabee* <../W/wannabee.html>`__; not hacker slang, but used among crackers, phreaks and `*warez d00dz* <../W/warez-d00dz.html>`__. Not as negative as `*lamer* <../L/lamer.html>`__ or `*leech* <../L/leech.html>`__.
