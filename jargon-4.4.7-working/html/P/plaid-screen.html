[XEROX PARC] A ‘special effect’ that occurs when certain kinds of `*memory smash* <../M/memory-smash.html>`__\ es overwrite the control blocks or image memory of a bit-mapped display. The term “salt and pepper” may refer to a different pattern of similar origin.
