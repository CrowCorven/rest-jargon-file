priority interrupt `Prev `__  P  `Next `__ -------------- **priority interrupt**: n. [from the hardware term] Describes any stimulus compelling enough to yank one right out of `*hack mode* <../H/hack-mode.html>`__. Classically used to describe being dragged away by an `*SO* <../S/SO.html>`__ for immediate sex, but may also refer to more mundane interruptions such as a fire alarm going off in the near vicinity.
