provocative maintenance `Prev `__  P  `Next `__ -------------- **provocative maintenance**: n. [common ironic mutation of preventive maintenance] Actions performed upon a machine at regularly scheduled intervals to ensure that the system remains in a usable state. So called because it is all too often performed by a `*field servoid* <../F/field-servoid.html>`__ who doesn't know what he is doing; such ‘maintenance’ often *induces* problems, or otherwise results in the machine's remaining in an *un*\ usable state for an indeterminate amount of time.
