phage `Prev `__  P  `Next `__ -------------- **phage**: n. A program that modifies other programs or databases in unauthorized ways; esp. one that propagates a `*virus* <../V/virus.html>`__ or `*Trojan horse* <../T/Trojan-horse.html>`__. See also `*worm* <../W/worm.html>`__, `*mockingbird* <../M/mockingbird.html>`__.
