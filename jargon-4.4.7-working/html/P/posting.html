posting `Prev `__  P  `Next `__ -------------- **posting**: n. Noun corresp. to v.: `*post* `__ (but note that `*post* `__ can be nouned). Distinguished from a ‘letter’ or ordinary `*email* <../E/email.html>`__ message by the fact that it is broadcast rather than point-to-point.
