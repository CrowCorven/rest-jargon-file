ping storm `Prev `__  P  `Next `__ -------------- **ping storm**: n. A form of `*DoS attack* <../D/DoS-attack.html>`__ consisting of a flood of `*ping* `__ requests (normally used to check network conditions) designed to disrupt the normal activity of a system. This act is sometimes called ping lashing or ping flood.
