paper-net `Prev `__  P  `Next `__ -------------- **paper-net**: n. Hackish way of referring to the postal service, analogizing it to a very slow, low-reliability network. Usenet `*sig block* <../S/sig-block.html>`__\ s sometimes include a “Paper-Net:” header just before the sender's postal address; common variants of this are “Papernet” and “P-Net”. Note that the standard `*netiquette* <../N/netiquette.html>`__ guidelines discourage this practice as a waste of bandwidth, since netters are quite unlikely to casually use postal addresses.
