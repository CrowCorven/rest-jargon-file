postcardware `Prev `__  P  `Next `__ -------------- **postcardware**: n. A kind of `*shareware* <../S/shareware.html>`__ that borders on `*freeware* <../F/freeware.html>`__, in that the author requests only that satisfied users send a postcard of their home town or something.
