whack `Prev `__  W  `Next `__ -------------- **whack**: v. According to arch-hacker James Gosling (designer of `*NeWS* <../N/NeWS.html>`__, `*GOSMACS* <../G/GOSMACS.html>`__ and Java), to “...modify a program with no idea whatsoever how it works.” (See `*whacker* `__.) It is actually possible to do this in nontrivial circumstances if the change is small and well-defined and you are very good at `*glark* <../G/glark.html>`__\ ing things from context.
