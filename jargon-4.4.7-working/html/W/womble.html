womble `Prev `__  W  `Next `__ -------------- **womble**: n. [Unisys UK: from British puppet-show characters] A user who has great difficulty in communicating their requirements and/or in using the resulting software. Extreme case of `*luser* <../L/luser.html>`__. An especially senior or high-ranking womble is referred to as Great-Uncle Bulgaria.
