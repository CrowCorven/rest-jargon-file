wizard hat `Prev `__  W  `Next `__ -------------- **wizard hat**: n. [also, after Terry Pratchett, pointy hat] Notional headgear worn by whoever is the `*wizard* `__ in a particular context. The implication is that it's a transferable role. “Talk to Alice, she's wearing the TCP/IP wizard hat while Bob is on vacation.” This metaphor is sufficiently live that one may actually see hackers miming the act of putting on, taking off, or transferring a phantom hat.
