wheel `Prev `__  W  `Next `__ -------------- **wheel**: n. [from slang ‘big wheel’ for a powerful person] A person who has an active `*wheel bit* `__. “We need to find a wheel to unwedge the hung tape drives.” (See `*wedged* `__, sense 1.) The traditional name of security group zero in `*BSD* <../B/BSD.html>`__ (to which the major system-internal users like `*root* <../R/root.html>`__ belong) is ‘wheel’.
