warez `Prev `__  W  `Next `__ -------------- **warez**: /weirz/, n. Widely used in `*cracker* <../C/cracker.html>`__ subcultures to denote cracked version of commercial software, that is versions from which copy-protection has been stripped. Hackers recognize this term but don't use it themselves.
