workaround `Prev `__  W  `Next `__ -------------- **workaround**: n. 1. A temporary `*kluge* <../K/kluge.html>`__ used to bypass, mask, or otherwise avoid a `*bug* <../B/bug.html>`__ or `*misfeature* <../M/misfeature.html>`__ in some system.
