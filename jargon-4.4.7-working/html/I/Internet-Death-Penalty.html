Internet Death Penalty `Prev `__  I  `Next `__ -------------- **Internet Death Penalty** [Usenet] (often abbreviated IDP) The ultimate sanction against `*spam* <../S/spam.html>`__-emitting sites — complete shunning at the router level of all mail and packets, as well as Usenet messages, from the offending domain(s).
