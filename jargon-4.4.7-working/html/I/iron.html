iron `Prev `__  I  `Next `__ -------------- **iron**: n. Hardware, especially older and larger hardware of `*mainframe* <../M/mainframe.html>`__ class with big metal cabinets housing relatively low-density electronics (but the term is also used of modern supercomputers). Often in the phrase `*big iron* <../B/big-iron.html>`__. Oppose `*silicon* <../S/silicon.html>`__.
