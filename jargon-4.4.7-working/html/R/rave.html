rave `Prev `__  R  `Next `__ -------------- **rave**: vi. [WPI] 1. To persist in discussing a specific subject. 2. To speak authoritatively on a subject about which one knows very little. 3. To complain to a person who is not in a position to correct the difficulty. 4. To purposely annoy another person verbally. 5. To evangelize. See `*flame* <../F/flame.html>`__. 6.
