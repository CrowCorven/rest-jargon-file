real estate `Prev `__  R  `Next `__ -------------- **real estate**: n. May be used for any critical resource measured in units of area. Most frequently used of chip real estate, the area available for logic on the surface of an integrated circuit (see also `*nanoacre* <../N/nanoacre.html>`__).
