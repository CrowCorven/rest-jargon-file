rainbow series `Prev `__  R  `Next `__ -------------- **rainbow series**: n. Any of several series of technical manuals distinguished by cover color. The original rainbow series was the NCSC security manuals (see `*Orange Book* <../O/Orange-Book.html>`__). These are now available `via the web `__. the term has also been commonly applied to the PostScript reference set.
