rude `Prev `__  R  `Next `__ -------------- **rude**: adj. 1. (of a program) Badly written. 2. Functionally poor, e.g., a program that is very difficult to use because of gratuitously poor (random?) design decisions. Oppose `*cuspy* <../C/cuspy.html>`__. 3. Anything that manipulates a shared resource without regard for its other users in such a way as to cause a (non-fatal) problem.
