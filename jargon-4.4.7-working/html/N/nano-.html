[SI: the next quantifier below `*micro-* <../M/micro-.html>`__; meaning ``× 10-9``] Smaller than `*micro-* <../M/micro-.html>`__, and used in the same rather loose and connotative way. Thus, one has `*nanotechnology* `__ (coined by hacker K. Eric Drexler) by analogy with microtechnology; and a few machine architectures have a nanocode level below microcode. Tom Duff at Bell Labs has also pointed out that “Pi seconds is a nanocentury”.
