nontrivial `Prev `__  N  `Next `__ -------------- **nontrivial**: adj. Requiring real thought or significant computing power. Often used as an understated way of saying that a problem is quite difficult or impractical, or even entirely unsolvable (“Proving P=NP is nontrivial”). The preferred emphatic form is decidedly nontrivial.
