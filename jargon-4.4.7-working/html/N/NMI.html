NMI `Prev `__  N  `Next `__ -------------- **NMI**: /N·M·I/, n. Non-Maskable Interrupt. An IRQ 7 on the `*PDP-11* <../P/PDP-11.html>`__ or 680[01234]0; the NMI line on an 80[1234]86. In contrast with a `*priority interrupt* <../P/priority-interrupt.html>`__ (which might be ignored, although that is unlikely), an NMI is *never* ignored.
