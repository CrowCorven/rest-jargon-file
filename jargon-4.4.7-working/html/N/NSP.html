NSP `Prev `__  N  `Next `__ -------------- **NSP**: /N·S·P/, n. Common abbreviation for ‘Network Service Provider’, one of the big national or regional companies that maintains a portion of the Internet backbone and resells connectivity to `*ISP* <../I/ISP.html>`__\ s. In 1996, major NSPs include ANS, MCI, UUNET, and Sprint.
