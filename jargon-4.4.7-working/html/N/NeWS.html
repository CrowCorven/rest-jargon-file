NeWS `Prev `__  N  `Next `__ -------------- **NeWS**: /nee´wis/, /n[y]oo´is/, /n[y]ooz/, n. [acronym; the “Network Window System”] The road not taken in window systems, an elegant `*PostScript* <../P/PostScript.html>`__-based environment that would almost certainly have won the standards war with `*X* <../X/X.html>`__ if it hadn't been `*proprietary* <../P/proprietary.html>`__ to Sun Microsystems. There is a lesson here that too many software vendors haven't yet heeded.
