not ready for prime time `Prev `__  N  `Next `__ -------------- **not ready for prime time**: adj. Usable, but only just so; not very robust; for internal use only. Said of a program or device. Often connotes that the thing will be made more solid `*Real Soon Now* <../R/Real-Soon-Now.html>`__. This term comes from the ensemble name of the original cast of *Saturday Night Live*, the “Not Ready for Prime Time Players”. It has extra flavor for hackers because of the special (though now semi-obsolescent) meaning of `*prime time* <../P/prime-time.html>`__.
