number-crunching `Prev `__  N  `Next `__ -------------- **number-crunching**: n. [common] Computations of a numerical nature, esp. those that make extensive use of floating-point numbers. The only thing `*Fortrash* <../F/Fortrash.html>`__ is good for. This term is in widespread informal use outside hackerdom and even in mainstream slang, but has additional hackish connotations: namely, that the computations are mindless and involve massive use of `*brute force* <../B/brute-force.html>`__. This is not always `*evil* <../E/evil.html>`__, esp. if it involves ray tracing or fractals or some other use that makes `*pretty pictures* <../P/pretty-pictures.html>`__, esp. if such pictures can be used as screen backgrounds.
