netlag `Prev `__  N  `Next `__ -------------- **netlag**: n. [IRC, MUD] A condition that occurs when the delays in the `*IRC* <../I/IRC.html>`__ network or on a `*MUD* <../M/MUD.html>`__ become severe enough that servers briefly lose and then reestablish contact, causing messages to be delivered in bursts, often with delays of up to a minute. (Note that this term has nothing to do with mainstream “jet lag”, a condition which hackers tend not to be much bothered by.)
