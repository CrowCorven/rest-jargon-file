toolsmith `Prev `__  T  `Next `__ -------------- **toolsmith**: n. The software equivalent of a tool-and-die specialist; one who specializes in making the `*tool* `__\ s with which other programmers create applications. Many hackers consider this more fun than applications per se; to understand why, see `*uninteresting* <../U/uninteresting.html>`__.
