tune `Prev `__  T  `Next `__ -------------- **tune**: vt. [from automotive or musical usage] To optimize a program or system for a particular environment, esp. by adjusting numerical parameters designed as `*hook* <../H/hook.html>`__\ s for tuning, e.g., by changing **#define** lines in C. One may tune for time (fastest execution), tune for space (least memory use), or tune for configuration (most efficient use of hardware).
