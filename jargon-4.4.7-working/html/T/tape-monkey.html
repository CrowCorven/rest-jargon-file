tape monkey `Prev `__  T  `Next `__ -------------- **tape monkey**: n. A junior system administrator, one who might plausibly be assigned to do physical swapping of tapes and subsequent storage. When a backup needs to be restored, one might holler “Tape monkey!” (Compare `*one-banana problem* <../O/one-banana-problem.html>`__) Also used to dismiss jobs not worthy of a highly trained sysadmin's ineffable talents: “Cable up her PC?
