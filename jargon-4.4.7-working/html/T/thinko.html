thinko `Prev `__  T  `Next `__ -------------- **thinko**: /thing´koh/, n. [by analogy with ‘typo’] A momentary, correctable glitch in mental processing, especially one involving recall of information learned by rote; a bubble in the stream of consciousness. Syn. `*braino* <../B/braino.html>`__; see also `*brain fart* <../B/brain-fart.html>`__.
