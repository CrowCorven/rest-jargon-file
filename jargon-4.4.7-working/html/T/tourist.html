tourist `Prev `__  T  `Next `__ -------------- **tourist**: n. 1. [ITS] A guest on the system, especially one who generally logs in over a network from a remote location for `*comm mode* <../C/comm-mode.html>`__, email, games, and other trivial purposes. One step below `*luser* <../L/luser.html>`__. ITS hackers often used to spell this `*turist* `__, perhaps by some sort of tenuous analogy with `*luser* <../L/luser.html>`__ (this usage may also have expressed the ITS culture's penchant for six-letterisms, and/or been some sort of tribute to Alan Turing). Compare `*twink* `__, `*lurker* <../L/lurker.html>`__, `*read-only user* <../R/read-only-user.html>`__. 2.
