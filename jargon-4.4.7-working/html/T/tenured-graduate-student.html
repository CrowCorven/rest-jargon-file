tenured graduate student `Prev `__  T  `Next `__ -------------- **tenured graduate student**: n. One who has been in graduate school for 10 years (the usual maximum is 5 or 6): a ‘ten-yeared’ student (get it?). Actually, this term may be used of any grad student beginning in his seventh year.
