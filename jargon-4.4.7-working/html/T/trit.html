trit `Prev `__  T  `Next `__ -------------- **trit**: /trit/, n. [by analogy with bit] One base-3 digit; the amount of information conveyed by a selection among one of three equally likely outcomes (see also `*bit* <../B/bit.html>`__). Trits arise, for example, in the context of a `*flag* <../F/flag.html>`__ that should actually be able to assume *three* values — such as yes, no, or unknown. Trits are sometimes jokingly called 3-state bits.
